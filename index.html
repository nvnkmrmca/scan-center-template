X-ray

Our skilled radiology team, advanced equipment, and commitment to patient care ensure that you receive accurate and timely results for your X-ray scans.

Our Diagnostic Center offers a comprehensive range of general X-ray services to examine various parts of the body, including bones, joints, chest, abdomen, and more. These X-rays help diagnose fractures, dislocations, infections, lung conditions, and other abnormalities.

CT Scan

we offer advanced CT (Computed Tomography) scan services to provide detailed cross-sectional images of the body.

Our state-of-the-art CT technology, combined with our experienced radiology team, ensures precise imaging and accurate diagnoses for a wide range of medical conditions.Trust our Diagnostic Center for accurate and comprehensive CT scans that contribute to your health care journey.

MRI Scan

we offer state-of-the-art MRI (Magnetic Resonance Imaging) scan services to provide detailed and comprehensive imaging of the body's internal structures.

Our advanced MRI technology, combined with our skilled radiology team, ensures precise and accurate diagnoses for a wide range of medical conditions.Experience the benefits of our advanced MRI scan services at our Diagnostic Center

PET Scan

we offer cutting-edge PET (Positron Emission Tomography) scan services to provide valuable insights into your body's metabolic activity and function.

Our advanced PET technology, combined with our experienced team of specialists, enables accurate diagnoses and plays a crucial role in the detection, staging, and monitoring of various medical conditions.

Endoscopy Scan

At our Diagnostic Center, we offer advanced endoscopy scan services to provide a detailed examination of internal organs and structures.

With our state-of-the-art endoscopic technology and experienced medical professionals, we ensure accurate diagnoses and personalized treatment recommendations.
